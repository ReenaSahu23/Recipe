Egg Curry

Ingredients

  • 4 boiled eggs
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Spices: turmeric, red chili powder, garam masala, salt

Instructions

  1. Heat oil and saut√© onions until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste, followed by tomato puree and spices.
  3. Cook until oil separates, then add boiled eggs.
  4. Simmer for 5 minutes, letting the eggs absorb the flavors.
  5. Garnish with coriander and serve hot with rice or roti.
