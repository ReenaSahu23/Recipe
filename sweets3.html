Barfi Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup condensed milk
  • 1 1/2 cups milk powder
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • Chopped nuts for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat ghee in a pan. Add condensed milk and milk powder. Stir continuously.
  2. Cook until the mixture thickens and leaves the sides of the pan.
  3. Add cardamom powder and mix well.
  4. Pour into a greased tray, flatten, and garnish with nuts. Let it cool and set, then cut into squares.
