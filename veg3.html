Veg Biryani Recipe

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice - 1 cup, soaked for 30 minutes
  • Mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, beans, potatoes) - 1 cup, chopped
  • Biryani masala - 2 tablespoons
  • Yogurt - 2 tablespoons
  • Saffron strands soaked in warm milk - 2 tablespoons

Instructions

  1. Cook rice until 70% done, then drain and set aside.
  2. Saut√© vegetables in oil, add biryani masala, yogurt, and cook until tender.
  3. Layer the vegetables and rice in a pot, drizzle saffron milk on top.
  4. Cover and cook on low heat for 15 minutes (Dum method).
  5. Fluff the biryani gently and serve hot.
