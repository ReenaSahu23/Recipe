Chhole Bhature Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chickpeas (soaked overnight)
  • 2 onions, 2 tomatoes (finely chopped)
    • tbsp ginger-garlic paste, 2-3 green chilies (slit)
  • 2 tbsp chole masala, 1 tsp cumin, 1 tsp turmeric, salt
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour, 1/2 cup yogurt, oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Pressure cook chickpeas with salt.
  2. Saut√© cumin, onions, ginger-garlic paste, and chilies; add tomatoes.
  3. Mix in spices, chickpeas, and water; simmer.
  4. Knead flour, yogurt, and water; rest dough
  5. Roll, fry dough; serve with chole
