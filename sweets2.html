Jalebi Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 2 tbsp cornflour
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp yeast
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup water (for syrup)
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, cornflour, turmeric, and yeast in warm water to make a batter. Let it ferment for 2 hours.
  2. Boil sugar and water to make a syrup. Keep warm.
  3. Fill batter in a piping bag and pipe spirals into hot oil. Fry until golden.
  4. Dip fried jalebis in warm syrup for a few seconds, then serve.
