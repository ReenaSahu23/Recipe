Pav Bhaji Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1 cup cauliflower, chopped
  • 1/2 cup green peas
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp Pav Bhaji masala
  • Salt, butter, oil

Instructions

  1. Boil and mash vegetables.
  2. Saut√© onions, ginger-garlic paste, tomatoes with spices.
  3. Add mashed vegetables, simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Serve hot with buttered pav.
