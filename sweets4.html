Kaju Katli Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cashew powder
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • Silver leaf (optional)

Instructions

  1. Make a syrup by boiling sugar and water until it reaches one-string consistency.
  2. Add cashew powder and cardamom powder to the syrup. Stir until the mixture thickens.
  3. Spread the mixture onto a greased surface and roll it out.
  4. Let it cool and set, then cut into diamond shapes. Decorate with silver leaf if desired.
