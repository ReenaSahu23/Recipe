Methi Paratha Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup whole wheat flour
  • 1/2 cup fresh fenugreek (methi) leaves, chopped
    • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/4 tsp carom seeds (ajwain)
  • Salt and water as needed

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, methi leaves, turmeric, ajwain, and salt.
  2. Gradually add water to form a soft dough; knead well.
  3. Divide dough into balls; roll each into a flat disc
  4. Cook on a hot griddle with oil until both sides are golden.
  5. Serve hot with yogurt or pickle.
