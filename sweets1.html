Gulab Jamun Recipe

Ingredients

  • Khoya - 1 cup
  • Paneer - 1/4 cup
  • Flour - 2 tbsp
  • Baking Powder - 1/4 tsp
  • Milk - 2 tbsp
  • Sugar - 2 cups
  • Water - 1.5 cups
  • Ghee - for frying

Instructions

  1. Mix khoya, paneer, flour, and baking powder to form a soft dough.
  2. Shape the dough into smooth balls.
  3. Heat ghee and fry the balls until golden brown.
  4. Soak in warm sugar syrup for 2-3 hours.
