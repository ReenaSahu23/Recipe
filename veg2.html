Palak Paneer Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups spinach (palak), chopped, 200g paneer (cottage cheese), cubed. 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed, 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste, 2 green chilies, 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder, 1/2 tsp garam masala, 1/2 tsp red chili powder, 2 tbsp cream (optional),Salt to taste, 2 tbsp oil or ghee

Instructions

  1. Boil spinach for 2-3 minutes and blend with green chilies into a puree.
  2. Heat oil, add cumin seeds, then saut√© chopped onions until golden.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste, tomato puree, and cook until oil separates.
  4. Mix in turmeric, red chili powder, salt, and spinach puree; cook for 5-7 minutes.
  5. Add paneer, garam masala, and optionally cream; cook for 2 minutes. Serve hot.
