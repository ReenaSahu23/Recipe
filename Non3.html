Fish Curry

Ingredients

  • 500g fish (cleaned and cut into pieces)
  • 2 onions (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric, 1 tsp red chili powder, salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Marinate the fish with turmeric and salt; set aside for 15 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, saut√© onions until golden brown, then add ginger-garlic paste.
  3. Add tomatoes, red chili powder, and cook until the tomatoes soften.
  4. Add water, bring to a boil, then gently add the marinated fish.
  5. Simmer for 10-15 minutes until the fish is cooked and the curry thickens.
